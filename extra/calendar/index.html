Frequently Asked Questions

How should I use the monthly Planner?
Its simple, you start off by creating your routine in the monthly planner, dont worry your data wont be lost until you manually delete through local storage keys or directly delete by using the GUI functions provided in the app.

In what order should I use this app?
You start off by making a monthly plan through our monthly planner feature, jot down additional points in the sticky notes for future reference. You can make a note of your to do list from the Daily Planner section, where we provide you with a kanban board to track your progress for the day. You can finally start executing your goals for the day by jumping to the Pomodoro timer feature, which times your work, also the notepad syncs the content from your Daily Planner, in case you want to refer to it, we also provide a simple to do list to keep track of the pomodoro goals. After your done with a pomodoro 25min session, you'll get a 5min break, you can refresh yourself at our meditation hub, which would run you through a series of breaths, infact you can even play some lofi music to meditate to, or you can listen to Queen's playlist at the chillout hub.
