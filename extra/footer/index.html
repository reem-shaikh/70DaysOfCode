A little about me.

We are mismatches between our physical reality and your perceptions, a countless people are in a delusion of what we really are, for they have countless perceptions of us and when it fails to meet the version they know, everyone suddenly becomes sceptic, that are we even real? Or just optical illusions? If only they knew.

Ruby

Meet my alter Ruby, a true introvert, she lives as my shadow, yet she repeatedly tries to convey how she didn’t choose the darkness, rather the darkness chose her, not many have witnessed her personally, for she prefers to hide away from this world, rather. because those who have, never accepted her; its like she’s her evil counterpart. She’s the artist in her, the writer, the critic, the split who believes love is a myth, pessimistic, sadist, phychopathic, nihilist.


Meet Rum, the insanely hyper split, something over the board social, the split who sees the light, maybe, who pulls her out of the darkness, the healer, the counsellor, the therapist, and a deep believer in the power of timing and fate.

People as I know, are both light and the darkness that follows it, only most of us identify as either. What we seem to forget is that were subconsiously seeking for a definite answer, its either white or black, the grey areas make us feel uneasy, and so we leave it unexplored. People as I know are both halo and horns, but they try to orient towards either, out of personal preference ofcourse. Just because you see light in someone's presence doesn’t necessarily mean that its their eternal truth. There's contours within everyone you'll ever meet, people hide things within themselves they would'nt dare tell anyone else, and it's really important to understand yours, mainly, to acknowledge it. Now know that, you are not your feelings, your feelings evolve, they are variable to change. you are a soul, the container of those feelings.

I believe in shadow work, in finding yourself through the process of writing, and I would want to encourage you to explore parts of your unhealed self, whether that's through art, contemplating, journalling or explore any form that lets you be you. I promise you, recovery.

Sending much love and power your way! Be ready to recieve it...
